# Project Proposal: Khan Chappals Website

Date: February 17, 2026

## Brand Name: Khan Chappals

## Summary
Khan Chappals is a traditional footwear brand known for premium handcrafted chappals. This project adds an educational angle by presenting the heritage, materials, and craftsmanship process alongside the product showcase. The website will help customers discover products while also learning about traditional Pakistani footwear making.

## Objective
- To build a trusted traditional footwear brand online.
- To educate visitors about chappal craftsmanship and heritage.
- To showcase premium handcrafted chappals in a clean layout.
- To improve customer inquiries and store visits.

## Product Categories
Khan Chappals offers a variety of traditional footwear, including:
- Peshawari style chappals.
- Kohati and Multani designs.
- Premium leather chappals with embroidery.

## Target Audience
- Men and young adults (18-45 years).
- Students and learners interested in local craftsmanship.
- Customers looking for traditional Pakistani footwear.
- Online shoppers seeking premium handcrafted products.
- Gift buyers and cultural event shoppers.

## Technology Stack
### Frontend
- HTML
- CSS

## Website Feature
- Responsive design.
- Navigation menu.
- Product showcase grid.
- About section (heritage and craftsmanship).
- Educational section (materials, tools, and process).
- Contact form and shop details.
- Homepage (hero, collection, and CTA).

## Customer Needs Addressed
- Easy access to product styles and pricing.
- Clear brand story and trust signals.
- Learning materials about traditional chappal making.
- Quick way to contact and visit the shop.
- Mobile-friendly browsing experience.

## Solution
Khan Chappals will have a lightweight website that:
- Highlights signature products with images and prices.
- Communicates brand heritage and craftsmanship.
- Educates visitors with a simple step-by-step process overview.
- Guides customers to contact or visit the store.
- Loads quickly and works well on mobile and desktop.

## Market Strategy
- Social media marketing for products and learning content.
- Short educational posts and reels about craftsmanship.
- Local search and Google Business updates.
- Seasonal promotions and featured collections.
- Customer reviews and testimonials.

## Project Scope (Small Project)
This is a small, focused website project designed to establish a professional online presence for Khan Chappals with an educational angle. The site will focus on product visibility, brand identity, learning content, and customer contact.

## Deliverables
- One responsive website (HTML + CSS).
- Branded homepage with hero, collection, and CTA.
- Product category highlights.
- Educational section on materials and process.
- About and contact sections with store details.
